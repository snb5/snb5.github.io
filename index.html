My Future Career Plans

Right now, I am a sophomore undergraduate Biology major with a Pre-Medicine concentration. When I graduate, I want to move on to medical school. Right now, I'm looking into schools like UVA, Pennstate, and Johns Hopkins for medical school. Since I plan on going to medical school, it is pretty clear that I want to become a physician. I'm not totally sure what kind of doctor I want to be yet. My main interests are family medicine and plastic surgery. I am interested in family medicine because it is the specialty I am most familiar with because that is the type of doctor I most often interact with/go see. I find plastic surgery very interesting and it is the specialty I enjoy learning about the most. However, I have heard that getting into plastic surgery is very difficult.

If I do not matriculate into medical school, I have some back-up plans. One of my back-up plans is to become a high school science teacher. Over winter break I shadowed my former high school biology teacher to get a feel for what her job is like. While being a doctor is still my primary goal, I think being a teacher could be interesting too. If I decided to become a teacher, I could get my master's in education after I get my undergraduate degree. Another back-up career I have in mind is a career in research. I already do undergraduate research and I like it well enough. However, it is not what I envision myself doing for the rest of my life, so this may just be a temporary career if I go this route.
